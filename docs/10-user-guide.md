# DreamNest User Guide

## What DreamNest Is

DreamNest is a goal-based money planning app that helps you save for meaningful life goals like:
- Wedding
- Home
- Travel
- Education
- Emergency fund

Instead of only tracking expenses, DreamNest helps you connect day-to-day money decisions to long-term dreams.

## How DreamNest Helps You

DreamNest helps you:
- Turn goals into clear savings plans
- Track income, expenses, and savings in one place
- See progress in simple percentages and timelines
- Understand how long a dream may take to complete
- Stay motivated with rewards, streaks, and milestones
- Continue your data across phone and desktop with the same account

## Who Should Use It

DreamNest is useful for:
- Students planning medium-term goals
- Working professionals saving monthly
- Couples planning shared goals
- Anyone who wants clarity and consistency in money habits

## Getting Started in 5 Steps

1. Create your account
- Open the app and go to Register
- Enter name, email, and password
- Log in

2. Create your first dream
- Open Dreams
- Click Create Dream
- Enter title, target amount, current saved amount, and monthly contribution
- Add deadline and priority if available

3. Track your money
- Open Monthly Savings or Transactions
- Add income entries
- Add expense entries
- Add savings to a specific dream

4. Review progress
- Check Dashboard for overall progress
- Open Dream Timeline to see milestones and estimated completion
- Use Analytics for trends and categories

5. Stay consistent
- Check in daily
- Keep updating transactions
- Adjust dream targets or contribution when needed

## Best Practices for Users

- Update entries on the same day to keep data accurate
- Use realistic monthly contribution values
- Review at least once a week
- Start with 1 to 3 goals, not too many at once
- Keep one primary high-priority dream

## Common Questions

## I logged in and my data is different. Why?

Make sure you are using the same email account on all devices. DreamNest syncs by account identity.

## Can I use this on both phone and desktop?

Yes. Login with the same account credentials and your data will sync.

## What if I entered a wrong amount?

Go to Transactions and edit or delete that entry.

## Is this app only for savings?

No. You can track income and expenses too, but the app is designed around goal completion.
