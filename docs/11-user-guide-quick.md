# DreamNest Quick Guide (Non-Technical)

This is a simple guide for everyday users.

## What DreamNest Does

DreamNest helps you save money for your life goals.

Examples:
- Wedding
- Home
- Vacation
- Education
- Emergency fund

It turns your goal into a plan and shows your progress clearly.

## Why It Helps

With DreamNest you can:
- See where your money goes
- Save with a clear monthly plan
- Track progress in one place
- Stay motivated with milestones
- Use the same account on phone and desktop

## Start in 3 Minutes

1. Create account
- Open Register
- Add name, email, password
- Login

2. Add your first goal
- Go to Dreams
- Click Create Dream
- Fill title, target amount, and monthly contribution
- Save

3. Add money updates
- Go to Monthly Savings or Transactions
- Add income
- Add expenses
- Add savings to your dream

Done. Your dashboard will update automatically.

## What to Use Daily

- Dashboard: quick overall progress
- Dreams: update each goal
- Monthly Savings: add daily/weekly entries
- Transactions: correct mistakes (edit/delete)
- Analytics: see your patterns

## Best Simple Routine

- Update entries every day or every 2 days
- Review progress every Sunday
- Keep one top-priority goal
- Increase monthly savings when possible

## Common Questions

## Can I use this on phone and laptop?

Yes. Use the same login credentials on both.

## I entered a wrong amount. What should I do?

Go to Transactions and edit or delete it.

## Is this only for savings?

No. You can track income and expenses too.

## One-Line Success Tip

Small, regular updates are more powerful than big, rare updates.
